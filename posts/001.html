Serpent Man

by Celestine Duru

As Moses lifted up the serpent so is the Son of man, last Adam (who became Sin) lifted up...The serpent in the Garden is Tongue of Adam-Man; Satan is Savoury things of Man. Jesus as Last Adam laid down Adam in his propagation hence destroyed Adam, serpent, satan man. Note also as serpent is subtle above all beast in field so Heart of Man is wicked &Evil above all... Where come Evil? It is not that God allows evil but Man who has the dominion over all including Evil allows it. God asked Adam, Where are you and Who told you are Naked and afraid? These generated the covering, veil of separation over Adam. God doesn't know and is not IN this realm of Sin (mistaken, missed Identity) and Death (separated, in another environment).


This is effect of or reality of Dream of Deep Sleep of Adam. Really Adam did not BELIEVE God (did God say...you shall not surely die, the tree, strange, imagined is able to make Adam be like god...i.e Adam of his own Will desired to be what he always IS image and likeness of God; sowed fig leaves as covering which exposes him to continued nakedness). God clothes us least we be found naked. Naked Spirits are in torment, torment of fear
